About Me

My name is Muhammad, and I'm a Software Engineer and a Surgical Neurophysiologist skilled in full stack web applications.
I have experience building beautiful RESTful APIs using NodeJS, ExpressJS, Python, and Flask as well as working with databases and external APIs, while making responsive frontend UIs with ReactJS.

I am currently seeking work that will allow me to grow and improve as an engineer while contributing my unique perspective and strong work ethic to your company mission.

As a Surgical Neurophysiologist for over 10 years, I thrive in fast paced, high stress environments. Being able to constantly solve problems as they happen, I learned that there is always more than one way to do anything. Keeping that in mind, I try to constantly iterate on my performance to improve my work. While my entire days and sometimes into the night were spent in the operating room, I poured hours daily into learning new technologies. I realized I have a passion for software development, and decided to make it into a career.
